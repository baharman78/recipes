Ingredients:
 - 1 package of ramen
 - 3 cups of boiling water
 - 1 seasoning package

Directions:
 1. Boil 3 cups of water
 2. Remove package and seasoning package from ramen
 3. Throw ramen into boiling water
 4. Throw in contents of seasoning package
 5. Drain water when noodles are soft
 6. Enjoy!
